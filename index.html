ABOUT

BTS - also known as Bangtan Boys (hangul: 방탄 소년단 [roman: Bangtan Sonyeondan]) - is a South Korean group composed of seven members: Jin, Suga, RM, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook. The group had its official debut on June 13, 2013, with the single "No More Dream", from the album "2 Cool 4 Skool".
